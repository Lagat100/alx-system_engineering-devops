Datadog

- Datadog is a monitoring and analytics platform for cloud-scale applications, providing monitoring of servers, databases, tools, and services through a SaaS-based data analytics platform. It allows developers, IT operations teams, and business users to see metrics, events, and performance of their entire stack in one place. With Datadog, users can monitor servers, databases, tools, and services, track the performance of their applications, and correlate events and metrics across the entire stack.
